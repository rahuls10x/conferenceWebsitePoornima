Welcome to the International Conference on
Materials, Manufacturing and Engineering Sciences 2026

The International Conference on Materials, Manufacturing and Engineering Sciences (ICMMES-2026) brings together researchers, academicians, industry experts, and students to share knowledge and explore innovations across key engineering fields. The conference focuses on four major tracks: Materials Science, Manufacturing Technologies, Engineering Sciences, and Interdisciplinary & Emerging Areas including AI, digital twins, biomedical devices, smart sensors, and green technologies. ICMMES-2026 provides a platform for presenting cutting-edge research, fostering collaboration, and advancing sustainable technological solutions.
